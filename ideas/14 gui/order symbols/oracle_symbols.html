Implementation Notes

Physical Cards: SVGs scale perfectly to 6mm (approximately 17 pixels at 72 DPI or 23 pixels at 96 DPI)

Line Width: stroke-width="4-5" in 100x100 viewBox = clean lines at small sizes

Colors: Customizable via stroke attribute - match to your card color scheme

Format: Plain SVG with no dependencies - works everywhere

CLI/TUI: Use the Unicode characters shown above (may need UTF-8 terminal support)
